Daily Math Challenge provides tools and experiences designed to help you build a consistent math practice habit. In providing these services, we may collect certain information about you. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use and protect that information, and the choices you have about your personal data.

By accessing or using the Service, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you should discontinue use of the Service immediately.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account, use interactive features, communicate with us, or otherwise use the Service. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and account credentials

When you access or use the Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:
